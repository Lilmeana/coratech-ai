Our Story

Coratech AI was founded on a single conviction: that the gap between AI hype and real business value is a delivery problem, not a technology problem. Too many organisations — especially growing businesses — invest in AI and see nothing. We exist to change that.

Our team brings hands-on experience building and shipping AI systems across a wide range of industries. We've worked with businesses of all sizes and understand the unique constraints that SMEs and startups face — from budget and bandwidth to team capability and data maturity.

Today, Coratech AI partners with businesses across multiple industries — from ambitious startups to established SMEs and beyond — delivering AI solutions that generate measurable, lasting impact without the enterprise price tag.
