Case Studies

Proof of Impact

Real projects. Real results. See how Coratech AI has transformed enterprises across industries.

Case Study 01

Multi-Agent Orchestration System for Financial Services

Problem

A leading investment bank's back-office operations were bottlenecked by manual data reconciliation across 12 disparate systems, taking 40+ hours per week per team.

Solution

We designed and deployed a multi-agent orchestration system where specialised AI agents each managed a data domain, with a master orchestrator handling routing, conflict resolution, and escalation logic.

Results

⬇️ 87% reduction in processing time
💰 £1.4M annual cost saving
⬆️ 99.6% accuracy rate
Case Study 02

AI Document Processing Automation for Legal Firm

Problem

A top-10 legal firm was spending thousands of billable hours on contract review, due diligence, and clause extraction — work that was error-prone and slow at scale.

Solution

We built a document intelligence pipeline using fine-tuned LLMs for clause identification, risk flagging, and summarisation — integrated directly into their existing document management system.

Results

⚡ 10× faster document review
🎯 94% clause extraction accuracy
🕐 3,200 hours saved per quarter
Case Study 03

SQL AI Agent for Enterprise Data Analytics

Problem

A retail group's non-technical teams were entirely dependent on the data team for every business question, creating a months-long backlog and slowing decision-making across departments.

Solution

We deployed a natural language-to-SQL AI agent connected to their enterprise data warehouse, allowing business users to ask questions in plain English and receive accurate, real-time analytics.

Results

🌎 500+ users with self-serve analytics
📊 78% reduction in data team requests
⏰️ Insights in seconds vs. days

About Us

Built for Enterprise Transformation

Coratech AI is a specialist AI consultancy founded by practitioners who have spent over a decade delivering real AI in complex enterprise environments.

Our Story

Coratech AI was founded on a single conviction: that the gap between AI hype and real enterprise value is a delivery problem, not a technology problem. Too many organisations invest in AI and see nothing. We exist to change that.

Our founders have held senior AI and data science roles at FTSE 100 companies, global banks, and AI-native tech firms. We have built, failed, learned, and shipped production AI systems at scale. That hard-won experience is what we bring to every client engagement.

Today, Coratech AI partners with enterprises across 9 industries globally — from seed-stage fintechs to multi-national corporations — delivering bespoke AI solutions that generate measurable, lasting impact.

🎯 Our Mission

To make enterprise AI accessible, effective, and transformative — delivering solutions that are secure, explainable, and deeply aligned with business outcomes.

🔭 Our Vision

A world where every organisation, regardless of size or sector, can harness the power of AI to solve their most important challenges and create extraordinary value.

⚡ Our Values

Integrity over hype. Evidence over assumption. Partnership over transaction. Outcomes over outputs. We hold ourselves accountable to the same metrics we set for our clients.
